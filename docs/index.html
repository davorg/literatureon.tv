Welcome to the alpha test version of Literature on TV.

Currently we're testing the look and feel of the site. This means that we don't actually have much data. (Hint: If you'd like to see the data that we do have, we recommend that you look into Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen.)

Literature on TV is your guide to screen adaptations of classic English literature — from BBC period dramas to big-screen Hollywood retellings. Whether you're tracking down a favourite version of Pride and Prejudice, comparing casts across adaptations of Great Expectations, or discovering a hidden gem you’d forgotten about, this site helps you explore the many ways great books have come to life on screen.

For each novel and play, you’ll find a list of TV and film productions — complete with cast details, production info, and handy links to buy the book or DVD if you'd like to add it to your collection.

Browsing the site helps you rediscover old favourites — and if you click through to Amazon using our links, you’ll be supporting the site at no extra cost. Thanks for visiting!
